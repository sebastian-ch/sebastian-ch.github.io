Map of Hydroelectric, Coal, and Nuclear Power Plants in the USA

This map was built with the Leaflet JavaScript library and displays hydroelectric, coal, and Nuclear power plants within the USA. Proportional symbols are used to show the power capacity of each plant. Clicking on the map will filter the plants to the ones within 500km of each click.

  • See the map: Power Plants in USA
  • Data Source: US Energy Information Administration

Map of Breweries in North America and Hawaii

This hexbin map shows the number of breweries in North America and Hawaii per 50km Inner Diameter Hexagons. QGIS was used to create this map.

  • See the map: Breweries in USA
  • Data Source: POI Factory

Starbucks or Dunkin' Donuts?

This bivariate map shows the ratio of Starbucks to Dunkin' Donuts throughout the USA. The analysis was completed in QGIS.

  • See the map: Starbucks or Dunkin' Map
  • Data Source: POI Factory
